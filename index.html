Who is Paul Huxen?

Paul is a bearded & bespectacled designer who is, when not quaffing copious amounts of tea, found designing interfaces for Burberry in jolly London town. He also uses his spare time to build a collection of fonts through Kraken Foundry.

How do I say hello?

Just throw a nice little email to hello@paulhuxen.co.uk

How's the site built?

This little fella runs on some fancy HTML5 and CSS which is souped up with the rather spiffy dynamic CSS language – {LESS}

Let The Kids Dance

Let The Kids Dance are a fashion company that take new graduates and talent from the worlds of design, art and fashion. My designs were to be used as part of the first women's clothing collection. The designs include previous work such as the typefaces Retro Disco and Fat Marker along with a new layout design for playing cards. New artwork was created especially for the collection such as the hand drawn slogan "…and so we cannot know ourselves…" and the Paisley Monster.
