Who is Paul Huxen?

Paul is a bearded & bespectacled designer who is, when not quaffing copious amounts of tea, found designing interfaces for Burberry in jolly London town. He also uses his spare time to build a collection of fonts through Kraken Foundry.

How do I say hello?

Just throw a nice little email to hello@paulhuxen.co.uk

How's the site built?

This little fella runs on some fancy HTML5 and CSS which is souped up with the rather spiffy dynamic CSS language – {LESS}

What can I say about Paul?

Paul has a pretty good beard, Paul wears some pretty stylish clothes, Paul's a bit of a creative genius online. Working with this man was a constant source of inspiration for me over the years. If your employment path crosses Paul's, don't pass up the opportunity to work with him. Craig Collie
