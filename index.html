Who is Paul Huxen?

Paul is a bearded & bespectacled designer who, when not quaffing copious amounts of tea, is found designing interfaces for Burberry in jolly London town. He also uses his spare time to build a collection of fonts through Kraken Foundry.

How do I say hello?

Just throw a nice little email to hello@paulhuxen.co.uk

How's the site built?

This little fella runs on some fancy HTML5 and CSS which is souped up with the rather spiffy dynamic CSS language – {LESS}. You can view the full website code on GitHub.

What can I say about Paul?

Paul has a pretty good beard, Paul wears some pretty stylish clothes, Paul's a bit of a creative genius online. Working with this man was a constant source of inspiration for me over the years. If your employment path crosses Paul's, don't pass up the opportunity to work with him. Craig Collie
Head of design, Mr Site

Where has Paul worked?

Burberry

February 2012 – Present

Working as part of the Digital Platforms Team I'm tasked with creating visually stimulating and engaging web interfaces. Notable contributions to the company include full artistic lead on a new checkout process, a mobile social network called "Burberry World" and preliminary work on mobile platforms.

Mr Site

May 2009 – February 2012

Over the years my role in the company developed and evolved and I’ve been fortunate enough to work on a wide variety of projects. These have included; A redesign of the company and product identities, website development & maintenance, product interfaces and creation of print advertising such as tube adverts, flyers and trade show stands.
