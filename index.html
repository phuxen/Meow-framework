Some say he's the 'Pusher of pixels', some say he's the 'Top hipster of typography', others say he mucks about for a living…

Paul Huxen is a bearded & (usually) bespectacled designer who, when not quaffing copious amounts of tea, is to found designing interfaces for Burberry in jolly London town. He also uses his spare time to build a collection of fonts through Kraken Foundry.

To say hello just write a nice little email to hello@paulhuxen.co.uk or throw a message on Skype – phuxen.

This website runs on some fancy HTML5 and CSS which is souped up with the rather spiffy dynamic CSS language – {LESS}. You can view the full website code on GitHub.

Burberry

Over the years my role in the company developed and evolved and I’ve been fortunate enough to work on a wide variety of projects.
