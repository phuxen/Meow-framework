I'm a designer, I create things…

…Always have done ever since I broke out a box of crayons and drew all over the walls. Thankfully now I don't draw on walls, I design for fashion labels or throw out a typeface through my font foundry – Kraken.

I'll be found creating a UI for a webmail service using HTML5, CSS3 and {LESS}, or knocking up some advertising and web banners in the Adobe CS5 suite.

I make good stuff

not shit stuff…

Stuff Item Other

I have a passion for design that makes me throw myself into any project that I receive and I make a brilliant cup of tea.

I've been in the design industry for 5 years and have a wide variety of skills in print and web design. Over the years I've built websites, made flyers, created a range of t-shirts, started a font foundry, tweaked and prodded web interfaces, designed trade show stands, laid out manuals and developed entire branding languages.

Let The Kids Dance

Let The Kids Dance are a fashion company that take new graduates and talent from the worlds of design, art and fashion. My designs were to be used as part of the first women's clothing collection. The designs include previous work such as the typefaces Retro Disco and Fat Marker along with a new layout design for playing cards. New artwork was created especially for the collection such as the hand drawn slogan "…and so we cannot know ourselves…" and the Paisley Monster.
